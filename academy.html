Makeup Academy

Nurturing the next generation of professional makeup artists

In 2021, we formally launched our Makeup Academy as an extension of Grandeur Artistry for wannabe make-up professionals who wish to pursue a career in the beauty industry. We look forward to truly leaving a mark in the beauty and wellness industry by nurturing young talents & like-minded entrepreneurs and transforming them into professional make-up artists.

Our USP is hands-on training from our Founder & Chief MUA Dr Shruthi, who is a cosmetologist and certified celebrity MUA with an experience of over a decade in the beauty industry. We have short and long-term courses with a designed curriculum and paid internship opportunities. Ever since its inception, we have had 20+ professional make-up artists who have successfully completed their training with us.
