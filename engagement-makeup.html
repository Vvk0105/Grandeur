Engagement Day Makeup

Engagement is the first pre-wedding ceremony in most cultures and you would definitely want to stand out as you step into the first phase of a new life journey.

When it comes to engagement makeup, it is meant to be different as it is neither as light as party makeup nor as heavy as bridal makeup. Based on your expectations, outfit, time and theme of the event, we help you decide the best engagement makeup look for you.

Our Expertise

Our makeup artists specialize in creating the perfect engagement look that enhances your natural beauty while complementing your outfit and jewelry. We understand that engagement photos last a lifetime, and we ensure you look flawless in every picture.

We Offer

  • Natural & Dewy Engagement Looks
  • Traditional Engagement Makeup
  • Contemporary Glam Styles
  • Outdoor/Indoor Appropriate Makeup
  • Makeup That Lasts All Day/Night

Our Process

We begin with a consultation to understand your vision, outfit colors, and personal style. Our team then creates a customized makeup plan that includes skin preparation, color matching, and setting techniques to ensure your makeup stays perfect throughout your special event.
