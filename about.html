About Grandeur

Premium bridal makeover studio and academy by Dr. Shruthi Vijayan

Grandeur by Dr Shruthi (Previously known as Grandeur Artistry) is a premium bridal makeover studio and academy launched in 2019 by celebrity makeup artist and aesthetic physician Dr Shruthi Vijayan. Located at Jawahar Nagar in Trivandrum, Grandeur offers state-of-the-art, premium makeup and grooming services for all occasions.

Grandeur's inception as a brand dates back to 2015 when our Founder & Chief MUA Dr Shruthi Vijayan began working as a freelance makeup artist purely out of a passion towards fashion, styling and makeup. In three years time, our clientele grew to 50+ happy brides, with Grandeur and Dr Shruthi earning a lot of attention in the beauty industry in Kerala.

The wholehearted support and motivation from our clients and well-wishers eventually helped us realise our biggest dream of our first brick-and-mortar store in December 2019, with premium wedding makeover and styling services. Having been in the beauty and wellness industry for a long time, we ensure world-class makeover services for our clients with absolute precision, offering you a stress-free experience on your big day.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional beauty services that enhance our clients' natural features and boost their confidence. We are committed to using premium products and techniques to deliver personalized makeover experiences for every special occasion.

Our Vision

To be the leading beauty destination in Kerala, recognized for excellence in bridal makeovers and professional makeup training. We aim to continuously innovate and set new standards in the beauty industry while nurturing the next generation of makeup artists.

Makeup Academy

In 2021, we formally launched our Makeup Academy as an extension of Grandeur Artistry for wannabe make-up professionals who wish to pursue a career in the beauty industry. We look forward to truly leaving a mark in the beauty and wellness industry by nurturing young talents & like-minded entrepreneurs and transforming them into professional make-up artists.

Our USP is hands-on training from our Founder & Chief MUA Dr Shruthi, who is a cosmetologist and certified celebrity MUA with an experience of over a decade in the beauty industry. We have short and long-term courses with a designed curriculum and paid internship opportunities. Ever since its inception, we have had 20+ professional make-up artists who have successfully completed their training with us.
