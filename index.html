Bridal Makeup Course

Kickstart your career as a makeup artist.

The course will generally focus on providing comprehensive training in bridal makeup techniques. It will cover both theory and practical aspects of bridal makeup artistry. The course may include various topics such as skincare, colour theory, makeup products and tools, face shapes, contouring, highlighting, eyeshadow application techniques, eyelash application, bridal makeup styles (e.g., natural, traditional, contemporary), long-lasting makeup techniques, and more. The course may also cover hairstyling for brides, including updos and accessorizing.
