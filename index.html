Grandeur Artistry

Grandeur Artistry is the culmination of years of love for the art of grooming. What started as an interest in fashion, grooming and makeup, eventually evolved into a vision, to ensure the best services in these fields.

With a wide range of hairstyling and grooming services offered by our dedicated and friendly staff, we ensure that all your grooming requirements are met in the best way possible.

What We Do

Bridal Makeup

Engagement Makeup

Engagement is a milestone worth cherishing forever. We use only premium-quality products and techniques to achieve long-lasting, photo-ready results.

Life Event Makeup

Advertising Makeup

From commercial shoots to print advertisements, we have the expertise to deliver flawless and striking makeup looks that perfectly align with your brand image.

Bridal Makeup Course

Kickstart your career as a makeup artist.

The course will generally focus on providing comprehensive training in bridal makeup techniques. It will cover both theory and practical aspects of bridal makeup artistry. The course may include various topics such as skincare, colour theory, makeup products and tools, face shapes, contouring, highlighting, eyeshadow application techniques, eyelash application, bridal makeup styles (e.g., natural, traditional, contemporary), long-lasting makeup techniques, and more. The course may also cover hairstyling for brides, including updos and accessorizing.
